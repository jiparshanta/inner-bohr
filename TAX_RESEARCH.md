## 1. Individual Income Tax (Personal Income Tax — PIT)

### Single Filers

| Taxable Income (NPR)       | Rate |
|-----------------------------|------|
| Up to 5,00,000              | 1% (Social Security Tax only) |
| 5,00,001 – 7,00,000        | 10%  |
| 7,00,001 – 10,00,000       | 20%  |
| 10,00,001 – 20,00,000      | 30%  |
| 20,00,001 – 50,00,000      | 36%  |
| Above 50,00,000             | 39%  |

### Married Couples (Joint Filing)

| Taxable Income (NPR)       | Rate |
|-----------------------------|------|
| Up to 6,00,000              | 1% (Social Security Tax only) |
| 6,00,001 – 8,00,000        | 10%  |
| 8,00,001 – 11,00,000       | 20%  |
| 11,00,001 – 20,00,000      | 30%  |
| 20,00,001 – 50,00,000      | 36%  |
| Above 50,00,000             | 39%  |

### Special Provisions
- **1% slab does NOT apply** to proprietorship income, pension income, or contribution-based pension fund income
- **Female taxpayer rebate:** 10% reduction on computed tax (employment income only)
- **Senior citizens (60+):** Additional NPR 50,000 exemption on taxable income
- **Disabled persons:** Additional 50% exemption on basic threshold
- **Non-residents:** Flat 25% on all Nepal-sourced income

### Deductions / Allowances (Residents)
| Category                          | Limit                        |
|-----------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Retirement/provident fund         | 1/3 of income or NPR 5,00,000 max |
| Life insurance premium            | NPR 40,000                   |
| Medical/health insurance          | NPR 20,000                   |
| Education loan interest           | Full amount deductible       |
| Remote area allowance             | NPR 10,000 – 50,000         |

## 4. Value Added Tax (VAT)

| Item                          | Detail            |
|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| Standard VAT rate             | **13%**           |
| Threshold (goods only)        | NPR 50 lakh/year |
| Threshold (services / mixed)  | NPR 30 lakh/year |
| Filing deadline               | 25th of following month |

### VAT-Exempt (Schedule 1)
- Agricultural products
- Food and basic necessities
- Medicines and health services
- Educational services
- Air and passenger transportation

### Zero-Rated (Schedule 2 — 0% VAT)
- Export of goods and services
- Supplies to Special Economic Zones (SEZs)
- Domestic machinery sold to hydropower projects

### Formula
```
VAT Payable = VAT on Sales − VAT on Purchases − Carried-forward VAT Credit
```

## 5. Tax Deducted at Source (TDS)

### Employment
- **Slab rate** (same as individual PIT slabs above)

### Service Fees & Investment Returns

| Category                                    | Rate  |
|---------------------------------------------|-------|
| Interest — foreign banks/institutions        | 10%   |
| Interest — domestic banks/institutions       | 5%    |
| Interest on deposits (natural persons)       | 5%    |
| Vehicle rental (VAT-registered)              | 1.5%  |
| Vehicle rental (non-VAT)                     | 2.5%  |
| Other lease/rent                             | 10%   |
| Royalty payment                              | 15%   |
| Service fees (VAT-registered provider)       | 1.5%  |
| Service fees (non-VAT provider)              | 15%   |
| Commission to non-resident by resident       | 5%    |
| Natural resource extraction rights           | 15%   |
| Retirement fund payments (approved/unapproved) | 5%  |
| Mutual funds / dividends / life insurance    | 5%    |
| Foreign university registration fees         | 5%    |
| Consultancy fees (VAT invoice)               | 1.5%  |
| Consultancy fees (non-VAT invoice)           | 15%   |
| Satellite / bandwidth / telecom usage        | 10%   |

### Windfall Gains
- Lottery and contest prizes: **25%**

### Contract Payments
- Single contract > Rs. 50,000 within 10 days: **1.5%**
- Insurance premium / reinsurance to non-residents: **1.5%**
- Contract payment to non-residents: **5%**

### TDS Exemptions
- Interest on loans (between entities)
- House rent to individual natural persons
- Mutual fund dividends (certain conditions)
- Insurance death benefits
- Payments by non-residents
- Personal expenses by natural persons
- Tax-exempt transactions

## 8. Social Security Fund (SSF)

| Contributor | Rate                    |
|-------------|-------------------------|
| Employee    | 11% of basic salary     |
| Employer    | 20% of basic salary     |
| **Total**   | **31% of basic salary** |

- Max taxable salary cap: NPR 3,50,000/month
- Deposit deadline: Within 15 days after month-end
- Mandatory for formal private sector and public enterprises

## Calculator Ideas for EzDarta

Based on this research, the following calculators would be valuable:

1. **Personal Income Tax Calculator** — Input: annual income, marital status, gender, age, disability → Output: tax payable
2. **Salary Tax Calculator** — Input: monthly salary, SSF contribution → Output: monthly/annual tax + take-home
3. **Corporate Tax Calculator** — Input: business sector, annual profit, location → Output: corporate tax payable
4. **VAT Calculator** — Input: sales amount → Output: VAT amount, total with VAT
5. **TDS Calculator** — Input: payment type, amount, VAT status → Output: TDS to deduct
6. **Capital Gains Tax Calculator** — Input: asset type, purchase/sale price, holding period → Output: CGT payable
7. **Business Registration Fee Calculator** — Input: company type, authorized capital → Output: total registration cost
8. **SSF Contribution Calculator** — Input: basic salary → Output: employee/employer/total contributions
9. **Small Business Tax Calculator** — Input: location, turnover, business type → Output: presumptive/turnover tax
10. **Freelancer Tax Calculator** — Input: income source (foreign/domestic), amount → Output: tax payable
